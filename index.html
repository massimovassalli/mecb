Mechanobiology

At the beginning of December 2024, a group of mechanobiology enthusiasts met in London, motivated by science, to discuss the opportunity to consolidate the Mechanobiology Community in Europe. A general interest emerged, together with several questions. If you want to contribute to shaping the future of the mechanobiology community in Europe, we invite you to fill in a simple form (link below), and to subscribe to the mailing list, to get updates on the next steps.
